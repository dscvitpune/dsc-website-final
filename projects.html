Digi Shopper (Shopping App)

Previous Next

Description

Digishopper is an online supermarket that enables customers to purchase their groceries, vegetables, and fruits easily. Once the client places an order, the supermarket through its established employees avails the products at the client’s doorstep at the stipulated time. Checkout option and Order amount status is offered across the App to make navigation hassle free. This feature gives them flexibility to buy their items with minimum screen navigations.
Main Features :

  • Realtime database
  • Two-tier authentication
  • Door to door delivery
  • Instant notifications
  • Product management at merchant side
  • Live Order status
  • Cart management

Notesmaker (Android App)

Previous Next

Description

Notesmaker, an App focused on cutting down long paragraphs and articles into short notes.
Working :-

  • Image input and cropping image
  • text detection and extraction using OCR model (Google Vision API)
  • Text summarization (TF-IDF algorithm)
Finally the summarized text is converted into a pdf file and stored locally as well as on the Firebase Cloud.
